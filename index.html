A Letter Never Sent

what i carry quietly, even when
i don’t say it anymore.

a collection of thoughts that never made it to you, but never stopped existing in me.

i don’t really know why i’m writing this now, but... i still think about it a lot.

sometimes more than i should.

there are nights where everything feels normal for a while, then suddenly i remember us again — not even the big moments, just the little things. your random stories. the way you’d call me out of nowhere just because you missed me. the way your voice sounded softer whenever you were sleepy. the comfort of knowing i had you at the end of every exhausting day.

and then reality settles in again.

that i was the one who damaged something that once felt so safe.

i think one of the hardest things for me is knowing that none of this had to happen. we were okay. we were genuinely happy. we had plans, dreams, routines, futures we talked about so naturally like they were already waiting for us. and sometimes i still catch myself thinking, “what if i just didn’t do that?” what if i had been more careful? more mature? more aware of what i already had in front of me?

maybe we would still be us right now.

maybe i’d still be the person you run to first whenever life gets heavy.

maybe you’d still look at me the same way.

that thought genuinely destroys me more than anything else.

because the truth is, i never wanted anybody else. even now, after everything, my heart still looks for you naturally. it still reaches for you first. and maybe that’s why this hurts so much — because losing the version of us we once had feels like losing a home i thought i’d stay in forever.

i know people say “if it’s meant to be, it’ll happen again”, but sometimes i’m scared. scared that maybe i ruined the only thing i was ever so certain about.

it also hurts to see you slowly become more careful around me.

seeing you hesitate before opening up.

seeing you hold yourself back when before, everything between us felt so easy and natural.

and i understand why. i really do. i can never blame you for protecting yourself after i gave you a reason to fear losing your peace. but no matter how much i understand it, it still hurts knowing i became part of the reason why your heart grew heavier.

there are times i wish i could go back and shake my past self. tell him to stop being stupid. tell him not to risk something so genuine for something meaningless. tell him that one careless moment could echo this painfully for months. heck even years.

because now i carry this quiet regret everywhere.

in random hours of the night. during long rides to bumfuck nowhere. while staring at old messages. while hearing songs that remind me of you.

i miss you so much.

not just your presence.

not just talking to you.

i miss being yours.

i miss being your boyfriend. i miss loving you openly without fear and hesitation. i miss the small routines we built together without realizing how special they were while we were living them. i miss hearing “goodnight bebi". i miss your clinginess. i miss being the person you depended on without second thoughts. i miss feeling needed by you in that way.

and sometimes i wonder if you miss me like that too.

not just as someone familiar.

but as the person you once chose every day.

there are moments where i try to convince myself to just quietly accept things as they are now. to stop hoping too much. to just be thankful that you’re still here somehow. but my heart betrays me every single time.

because deep down, i still yearn for us.

i still yearn for the possibility that maybe one day we’ll find our way back to each other properly. healthier. softer. more certain. i still catch myself imagining what it would feel like if one day you looked at me again without fear attached to it.

and maybe that’s selfish of me.

but loving you has never really left me.

even now, when things are uncertain.

even now, when i know i have no right to ask for anything.

even now, when i’m trying to become better quietly.

you are still the person my heart chooses.

and if i’m being honest with myself, i think a part of me will always mourn the version of us that existed before everything changed. because that version of us was one of the happiest things i’ve ever experienced in my life.

you loved me so genuinely.

so fully.

so patiently.

and i’ll forever be sorry for hurting a heart that only ever wanted to love me sincerely.

but despite all the regret i carry, despite all the longing, despite how badly i still wish things could return to the way they were… one thing i know for sure is this:

always.

even in moments where i’m no longer beside you the way i used to be.

even if life eventually takes us in different directions.

even if someday i become just another chapter in your life.

a part of me will always quietly pray that you are loved gently, understood deeply, and protected from the kind of pain you never deserved to carry.

and selfishly, i still hope that maybe — just maybe — i could someday become that person for you again.

i love you.

i loved you in the past. i love you now. and i think some part of me will continue loving you for a very long time.

— Gi
